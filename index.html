About Me

Everybody needs a hero

My childhood hero is Wile E. Coyote, Genius. He never quit. He never gave up. He dreamed big dreams and believed in himself. He taught me perseverance, commitment, and dedication. Unfortunately, he had a terrible habit of doing things the hard way and that led me to seek other heroes who worked smarter not harder.

A bit of a rolling stone, I am grateful to have experienced many different cultures and places in my travels through most states and beyond the borders of this country. I love the mountains, the sea, and sleeping under stars. For ten years I owned and operated a small outdoor outfitter in Northern California, Adventures Unlimited. We specialized in custom whitewater expeditions and guided fishing trips, on the rivers of the Pacific Northwest. In 2003 I moved back east to be closer to family and start a family of my own. I am now happily settled in Rhode Island.

For 13 years, I was a professional boatbuilder. Working primarily with carbon fiber in composite construction, building custom sailing, ocean racing yachts, and some special projects. I have always been fascinated with technology and how things work. Then I discovered that I have an affinity for web development. I signed on for a 14-week web development immersive boot camp and was delighted to have found a new and exciting medium to work with. The possibilities and opportunities in this field are truly endless.


When I'm not staring at the screen, you'll find me on a boat relaxing or racing. Enjoying time with my kids, while they still enjoy me, or maybe frustrated on a golf course.


I am a life-long learner, constantly keeping busy in pursuit of challenges and always developing.
